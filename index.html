Birds

Sparrows Finches


There are all kinds of birds to go out and find, even in your backyard! It's easy to sit outside and hear the bird calls on a nice day.




Sparrows


Sparrows are the easiest bird to find across Missouri. There are at least three species of sparrow in our state. There are house sparrows, white-throated sparrows, and eurasian sparrows. All of these species you can find year-round!




Finches


Finches are another common backyard bird. There are a few known species found in Missouri. Those are the house finch, the pine siskin, the American goldfinch, and the purple finch. Most of these species are found year-round.




Those are some common birds in Missouri!
